## Overview

The **WOLFSBURG AWARD for Urban Vision** is a biennial open ideas competition hosted by the City of Wolfsburg. It invites European university students to submit innovative concepts across architecture, neighbourhood planning, mobility design, and related fields.

---

## Eligibility

Students enrolled at European universities, colleges, or universities of applied sciences in:
- Architecture
- Interior design
- Landscape architecture
- Urban planning
- Monument preservation
- Fine arts
- Related disciplines

Both individual students and university courses may participate. University courses are registered by their supervisor.

---

## Theme

> "No specifications for the theme or design area. Everything is welcome."

The competition seeks contributions that examine Wolfsburg's architectural quality and unique history. Experimental approaches are encouraged.

---

## Prize

Total prize pool: **5,000 EUR** distributed among winners.
