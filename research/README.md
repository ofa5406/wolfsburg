> Auto-research the project end to end — urban & mobility design, real-world examples, behavioral shift & sociology, future projections, supportive data & arguments, project-execution workflow (incl. AI & non-Claude agents), strategy, weak points + solutions + action plans, transfer-hub typologies/elements/toolpalette and the logic of their urban configuration, the Rhino workflow + recommendations, and a realistic plan — all reusable and persistent.

## How to use the library

- **Prepping for June 25?** Start with `06` (arguments) and `07` (weak points), then `03` (hubs) and `09` (workflow plan).
- **Writing the competition narrative?** Pull from `01`, `04`, `05`, `08`.
- **Need a citable number fast?** Search `sources.md` or the "Sources" section of the relevant doc.
- **Defending the hub design?** `03` explains the *why* behind the toolpalette and tier logic.

## Maintenance

This is a living library. When you learn something worth keeping, add it to the right document and append the citation to `sources.md`. The persistence rule lives in the project `CLAUDE.md` (Start/End-of-Session protocol) and `HANDOFF.md`.
