# Alternative 2 — Production Checklist

Lighter on tech, heavier on logistics than Alt 1. Shared items (loop build, card/ticket
prints, QR verification, `[CARS-REPLACED]` recompute) are in
[`../alt-1-stations/production-checklist.md`](../alt-1-stations/production-checklist.md) —
do those regardless of which alternative wins.

## Week 1 — decide & source (by Fri July 3)

- [ ] Team decision: Alt 1, Alt 2, or the combo (Alt 2 space + Alt 1 evening projection).
- [ ] Equipment request (smaller): 1 TV/monitor + 1 pool PC + 1 tablet. Mention we're
      *releasing* both projectors + 2 tablets to the other projects.
- [ ] **Furniture hunt:** 1 café table (Ø ~0.7 m), 3 chairs (mismatched fine), 2–3 pots
      + one small potted tree, 1 bicycle + helmet, 1 wooden crate, cheap rug.
      Sources: campus café, Werkstatt, team flats, botanical corner. Confirm by Jul 6.
- [ ] Verify the `[CARS-REPLACED]` figure AND the "~12,500 spaces" claim on the manifest
      board against the parking inventory data (`sources/data_sources.md`) — print nothing
      unverified.
- [ ] Check pool rules: floor tape OK? chalk-on-board OK? props with "drinks" OK?

## Week 2 — build & print (July 6–8)

- [ ] Chalk field: dark hardboard/black paper roll cut to 2.0 × 1.25 m + white/coloured
      chalk (charcoal sticks only if fixative is allowed indoors) + chalk cup for the crate.
- [ ] Print: manifest board (A3), menu cards (2 copies + take-away stack), labels §5
      (bike tag, pot tag, car-shadow text, tablet tent card, crate sign), chalk invitation.
- [ ] Everything shared: cards, tickets, title poster, loop — per Alt 1 checklist.
- [ ] Tablet holder + cable lock; screen stand or table for the monitor.

## Setup day (Wed July 8)

1. Tape the 5.0 × 2.5 m rectangle (accent tape).
2. Tape the **car shadow** (4.5 × 1.8 m outline, thin white tape) inside it.
3. Furnish: screen at the head end → chalk field centre-left → café set on the "car" →
   bike + pots along the long edge → crate by the chairs
   (plan: [`../diagrams/07_parking-space-inventory.svg`](../diagrams/07_parking-space-inventory.svg)).
4. Seed the chalk field (hopscotch + flower + crossed-out car).
5. Dress the table: menu holder, napkin-holder tickets, tablet, prop cups, one plant.
6. Mark the photo position (tape cross), take the Day-0 photo.
7. Unattended dry run — 10 min out of the room; then a cold visitor test.

## Daily (July 9–12)

- Open: loop running · tablet charged & in kiosk mode · restock crate · straighten chairs
  *(but not too much — a lived-in table invites sitting)*.
- Evening: the 3-photo script (`draft-content.md` §7) · fixative on charcoal if used ·
  swap chalk paper when full (keep full sheets flat — they're documentation).

## Fallbacks

| If… | Then… |
|---|---|
| No table/chairs found by Jul 6 | Fall back to Alt 1 (needs no furniture) — this is the main go/no-go |
| No TV/monitor granted | Loop on the tablet at the table; personas via tickets only |
| Chalk forbidden in the pool | Paper field + thick pencils/markers tied on strings |
| Space too small in the real room | Shrink to a 4.0 × 2.0 m "compact car" space — adjust manifest numbers |
